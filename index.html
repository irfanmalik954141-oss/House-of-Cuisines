Our Story

A Canvas of Flavors & Heritage

Welcome to House of Cuisines, Jammu's most celebrated destination for fine dining. Nestled in the heart of the Old Heritage City, we offer a harmonious blend of North Indian, Chinese, and authentic Kashmiri culinary traditions.

Our spacious, family-friendly setting boasts a vibrant yet sophisticated atmosphere. Perfect for intimate dinners, family gatherings, or grand celebrations, we pledge a meticulous focus on taste, premium ingredients, and impeccable service to bring you a truly elevated dining experience.
